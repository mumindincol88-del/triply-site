Staying only in all-inclusive

Lara all-inclusive hotels are good value but you miss everything. Kaleiçi, the boat tours, the ruins — all need you to leave the resort. Plan at least 2 day trips.

Visiting in July–August

Peak summer is brutal heat (40°C+) and massive crowds. May, June, September and October give perfect weather, lower prices and much less tourists.

Skipping the boat tour

The Blue Cave and Suluada (Turkish Maldives) boat tour is the best day out from Antalya. Many visitors leave without doing it — don't be one of them.
