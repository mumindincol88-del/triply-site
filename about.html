About Triply

Trip planning,
without the tab chaos

Triply was built to solve one frustration: planning a city trip means opening 15 tabs and losing track of everything. We put it all on one page.

What is Triply?

Triply is a city trip planning platform that brings together everything you need for a trip — flights, hotels, tours and restaurants — on one focused page per destination.

We don't handle payments. Instead, we help you discover, compare and decide — then send you to trusted platforms like Booking.com, Skyscanner, Viator and TheFork to complete your booking safely.

Think of Triply as your well-travelled friend who knows each city well and gives you honest, structured advice — without trying to sell you anything directly.
