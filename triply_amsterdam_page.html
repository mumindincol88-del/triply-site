Getting around Amsterdam

Use a contactless bank card — it works on all trams, metro and buses in Amsterdam. Or buy a GVB multi-day pass at Schiphol or any metro station.

Booking on Dam Square

It looks central but it's surrounded by tourist restaurants and overpriced hotels. Jordaan or De Pijp gives a much better experience for the same price.

Skipping free museums

Stedelijk Museum (modern art) and Amsterdam Museum are free or discounted with the I Amsterdam City Card. Most visitors miss the Stedelijk entirely — don't.
