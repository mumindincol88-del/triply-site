Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 24, 2026

Summary: Triply does not collect personal data beyond basic analytics. We do not handle payments. Bookings happen on trusted third-party platforms. We use affiliate links to earn commissions at no extra cost to you.

1. Who we are

Triply ("we", "us", "our") operates the website triply.info — a city trip planning platform. We help travelers plan trips by providing destination guides, then directing them to trusted booking partners.

Contact: info@triply.info

2. Information we collect

We collect minimal data to operate and improve the platform:

  • Analytics data: Page views, session duration, device type and geographic region — collected anonymously via Google Analytics. No personally identifiable information is stored.
  • Contact form submissions: If you contact us via our contact form or email, we store your name and email to respond to your enquiry.
  • Cookies: We use essential cookies for site functionality and analytics cookies. See Section 6 for details.

We do not collect payment information, passport details or sensitive personal data. All bookings are completed on external partner platforms.

3. How we use your information

  • To provide and improve the Triply platform
  • To understand how users interact with our city guides
  • To respond to enquiries sent to info@triply.info
  • To comply with legal obligations

We do not sell, rent or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Affiliate links and third-party platforms

Triply uses affiliate links to earn commissions when you click through to and book on partner platforms including:

  • Booking.com
  • Skyscanner
  • Viator
  • Klook
  • TheFork

When you click an affiliate link and complete a booking, we may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. The price you pay is the same whether you use our link or visit the platform directly.

These third-party platforms have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them before making a booking.

5. Data sharing

We share data only in the following circumstances:

  • Analytics providers: Anonymised usage data with Google Analytics
  • Legal requirements: If required by law or legal process
  • Business transfers: In the event of a merger or acquisition

6. Cookies

We use the following cookies:

  • Essential cookies: Required for the website to function. Cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics cookies: Google Analytics — help us understand how visitors use the site. You can opt out via your browser settings or Google Analytics Opt-out.
  • Affiliate cookies: Set by partner platforms when you click through to book. These track referrals for commission purposes.

7. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to access your data
  • Right to correct inaccurate data
  • Right to delete your data
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to data portability

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@triply.info.

8. Data retention

We retain contact form submissions for up to 12 months. Analytics data is retained according to Google Analytics default settings (26 months). We do not retain payment or booking data as all transactions occur on third-party platforms.

9. International transfers

Triply operates from Turkey. If you access our site from the European Union or other regions with data protection laws, please note that your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your jurisdiction. We take appropriate measures to protect your data in accordance with applicable law.

10. Children's privacy

Triply is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected data from a child, please contact us at info@triply.info.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of Triply after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact us

For any privacy-related questions or requests:

  • Email: info@triply.info
  • Website: triply.info/contact
