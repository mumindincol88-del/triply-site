Underestimating distances

Sydney is huge. Bondi to the Blue Mountains is 2 hours. Plan by zone — don't try to cross the city every day.

Missing the ferry network

Sydney's ferry system is one of the world's most scenic commutes. The Manly ferry and Parramatta River ferry are tourist experiences in themselves — and they're just A$8.

Visiting only tourist areas

Newtown, Glebe and Surry Hills are where Sydney actually lives. One dinner in Surry Hills will show you the food scene that travel writers fly here for.
